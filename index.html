I am Silas Cundiff, and this website was designed with the meaning of my name as inspiration. My first name means "of the forest", and the Cundiff family originally used a Coat of Arms.


I graduated from Southern Crescent Technical college where I majored in Computer Programming and Web Design & Development.
I began Computer Programming, but when I took the required Website Development class, a passion for web design took root in me.


From a young age, I have always loved making beautiful designs in games for the enjoyment of myself and others. So, when I discovered I could use that creativity alongside my other interest in programming, I immediately decided to dual-enroll.


The design process is a lot of fun for me, starting with just the seed of an idea and building it into something I can be proud of. I put a lot of thought into every project I work on, and I hope every design decision of this website shows that.
